A brief timeline: I was born in San Jose, California and moved to Gilbert, Arizona during elementary grade school. I then attended Arizona State University for my undergrad and then moved to Columbus, Ohio for the graduate phd program at the Ohio State University.

I enjoy hiking at national parks and skiing. The picture above was taken at the one sad hiking destination in the flatlands of Ohio: Hocking hills. Below, is a picture I took while hiking the Iceberg Lake trail at Glacier National Park in summer 2021. Growing up in Arizona, I have been spoiled with a wide variety of beautiful hiking options.

You can usually find me at some coffee shop in Columbus, I frequent Sweetwaters at Dublin Bridge Park and Staufs in Grandview. I like to watch japanese anime (Attack on Titan, Steins;Gate, The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya are my top 3) and documentaries/shorts on gastronomy (like Chef’s Table on Netflix, and NYC Eater’s Omakase series on YouTube in particular). I am trying to learn piano. I dabbled for around two months in my undergrad but never learned a single song, and I picked it up again in March 2021 as quarantine hobby.

One of my favorite quotes:

Not everyone can be a truffle. Most of us are potatoes. And a potato is a very good thing to be.

  • Massimo Bottura, Osteria Francescana (3 Michelin Stars from 2011-present)

I am definitely a potato and am happy to be be one.

Lastly, I suppose it must be mentioned that my favorite animal is the capybara, the world’s largest rodent. I had the chance to see and pet a few capys at the Kobe Animal Kingdom in 2018 and 2019.

Here is video of a few capybaras taking a hot outdoor bath. One manages to balance a mandarin on its head! かわいい!
