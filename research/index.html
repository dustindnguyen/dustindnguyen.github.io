Research

 2021-09-15  120 words  No tag

I work in theoretical astrophysics focused on the physics and phenomenology of galactic starburst winds. For numerical studies, I use the massively-parallel GPU code, cholla, on the Pitzer cluster at the Ohio Supercomputer Center to run high resolution 3-dimensional hydro simulations.

Research interests:

  • supernovae driven outflows
  • hydrodynamic instabilities
  • X-ray astrophysics
