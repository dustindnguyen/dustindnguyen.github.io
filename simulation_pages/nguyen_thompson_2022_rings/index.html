summary

Below, are embedded .gif files for Simulations \( \texttt{A} \) and \(\texttt{A}_\star \) presented in my recent paper: Nguyen & Thompson, 2022. The first set are 2d density, temperature, velocity, and entropy slices. The second set are rotated 3d temperature-weighted renders. The temperature weighting colors the hot \( 10^7 \)K component red, and the cooler \( 10^4 \)K component blue. The cool component transparency is signifigantly lowered such that the warm-hot structrure reamins prominent when rendered.
